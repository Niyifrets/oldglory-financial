How do I access my money or make a deposit? +
As an account holder, you may pay your expenses with an Old Glory Bank debit card and online bill pay. You can withdraw cash for free from one of over 40,000 in-network ATMs, which you can easily find using our ATM locator in our mobile app and website. Of course, your Old Glory Bank Debit Card will work at almost every ATM on the planet, it just means you may have to pay a small fee (e.g., $3.50), if it's not one of our free ATMs. Please note: In some cases, the owner/operator of an ATM may impose withdrawal limits that are less than what Old Glory Bank allows. Another easy way to withdraw cash is to use your debit card at your local grocery or convenience store and request cash back. Need to deposit cash into your Old Glory Bank account? Our customers love our Cash-IN feature! With Cash-IN, you can deposit cash by visiting one of over 88,000 participating retailers - show them a code in your app, hand them the cash, and the money is credited to your account within minutes! Deposit checks anytime, anywhere, using our mobile check deposit feature in our app.

Is it safe to use my Old Glory Bank debit card? Where can I use my debit card? +
Your Old Glory Bank debit card is accepted at any location where Mastercard® is accepted, which is just about everywhere. Using your Old Glory Bank Debit card is generally as safe as a credit card, unless you ignore your account and let the fraud linger (which you should never do). If you notify us within 2 business days of your debit card being lost or stolen, or within 2 business days of someone using your debit card fraudulently, you are generally responsible for only the first $50 (just like most credit cards). If, for some reason, you don't notify us within the first 2 business days and wait up to 60 days (which you should never do), then you are generally responsible for only the first $500 (just like most credit cards). If you completely ignore the fraud and never inform us, then you may be responsible for the full loss, but don't let that happen. Please stay aware of your account activity. One of the reasons why we LOVE our Old Glory Bank technology is because you get notified on your phone (and your smartwatch, if you choose) each time your Old Glory Bank Debit Card is used. Thus, you should never be surprised about a fraudulent charge. If for some reason, you don't have your phone alerts turned on (we strongly recommend that you do), then merely go into our Card Manager feature within your Old Glory Bank App and look at your card activity. Not only does our great technology show you each charge, but it also shows you a logo of the merchant and the location. Thus, you should never be confused as to whether you made a charge. Also, please remember that you can easily turn your Old Glory Bank Debit Card "on and off" using our Card Manager feature, so that if you leave behind your debit card somewhere safe, you can turn it "off" until you retrieve it. That way, you don't need to cancel your debit card and get a replacement card (unless it's actually lost or stolen). Just wait until it's back in your possession and then turn it back on. You can use your Old Glory Bank Debit Card with confidence, so make a statement and proudly use your Old Glory Bank Debit Card everywhere!
